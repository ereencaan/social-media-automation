Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Hitratech Solutions Ltd ("we", "us", "Hitrapost") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the Hitrapost service at hitrapost.co.uk. We comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

Hitratech Solutions Ltd is a private company limited by shares, incorporated in England & Wales. We operate Hitrapost — a B2B SaaS platform that generates AI social-media content and captures leads from websites, live chat, and inbound email for small and medium-sized businesses.

  • Trading name: Hitrapost
  • Legal entity: Hitratech Solutions Ltd (England & Wales)
  • Service URL: https://hitrapost.co.uk
  • Privacy queries: privacy@hitrapost.co.uk

2. What personal data we collect

From you (the account holder)

  • Account data: name, email, organisation name, password hash (we never store the plain password).
  • Profile & brand data: business industry, target audience, tone of voice, logo image, contact details you publish in your captions (phone, WhatsApp, Instagram handle).
  • Authentication & security: two-factor authentication tokens (where enabled), session cookies, login timestamps, IP addresses for rate limiting and fraud prevention.
  • Billing data: name, billing address, and last 4 digits of the payment card — collected and stored by Stripe (our payment processor). We never see, transmit, or store full card numbers, CVV codes, or full expiry data.

From your customers (lead data you ingest)

If you use Hitrapost's lead-capture features — webhook intake, Tawk.to or other live-chat connectors, email-to-lead, Meta DM connectors — your prospects' details (name, email, phone, message body, timestamps) are stored against your workspace. You are the data controller for that information; Hitrapost acts as data processor under UK GDPR Article 28.

Generated automatically

  • Service usage counters: posts created, AI calls made, leads captured (totals per billing cycle, for quota enforcement).
  • Logs: request paths, status codes, latencies, error traces — for security monitoring and debugging. Retained for 90 days.

5. How long we keep data

  • Account & profile data: for the lifetime of your subscription, plus a 30-day grace period after deletion (during which the account can be restored).
  • Lead data you ingest: retained as long as your account is active; deleted with the account.
  • Billing records (invoices, receipts): 6 years after the last transaction (UK statutory retention for tax purposes).
  • Logs: 90 days.
  • Backups: rolling 30 days, encrypted at rest.

6. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Port your data to another service in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent (where consent is the lawful basis)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@hitrapost.co.uk. We respond within 30 days as required by UK GDPR.

7. Cookies

Hitrapost uses only essential session cookies required for authentication and CSRF protection. We do not run third-party advertising trackers, behavioural analytics pixels, or session-recording tools by default. If you opt-in to product analytics in the future, this policy will be updated with full disclosure and you will be able to withdraw consent from Settings.

8. Security

We protect your data with:

  • HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) for all traffic in transit
  • AES-256 encryption at rest for the database and backups
  • Scoped, HttpOnly, SameSite=Lax session cookies
  • Per-IP rate limiting on auth and intake endpoints
  • HMAC signature verification on all webhook endpoints (Meta, Stripe, Tawk)
  • Optional two-factor authentication (TOTP) for all paid plans
  • Strict per-organisation row-level scoping in the database
  • Daily off-site encrypted backups

No system is 100% secure. Please use a strong unique password and enable 2FA. Report security concerns to security@hitrapost.co.uk.

9. International transfers

Some sub-processors are based outside the UK (notably the US). For each such transfer we rely on either a UK Adequacy decision, the UK International Data Transfer Agreement, or the UK Addendum to the EU Standard Contractual Clauses, ensuring GDPR-equivalent protection.

10. Children's data

Hitrapost is a business-to-business service. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe we hold a child's data, please email privacy@hitrapost.co.uk and we will delete it.

11. Automated decision-making

We use AI models to draft, review, and grade social-media content and lead emails. These tools assist human decision-making but do not autonomously make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on you. You always retain the right to review, edit, or reject AI-generated output before it is published.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be notified to active customers by email at least 14 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
